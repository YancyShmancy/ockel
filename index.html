Ockel Sirius B

We’re thrilled to introduce The Ockel Sirius B - A pocket PC as powerful as a desktop and as portable and light as a smartphone. Sirius B fits right in your pocket, is as powerful as a desktop, and as light as your smartphone.

Specs Don't Lie

  • Windows 10
  • Intel Atom Bay-trail Z3735F Quad Core Processor
  • Intel HD Graphics
  • 2GB 1333MHz DDR3 RAM
  • 32GB eMMC Flash Extended Internal Storage

Eye-Catching Design

You shouldn’t judge a book by its cover, but at Ockel we are passionate about good design so we want our products to look stunning. That’s why Sirius B has an attractive white housing with aluminium edges.

Seriously Small

Ockel Sirius B easily fits into your pocket or bag. With a size close to smartphones such as the iPhone 6 and Samsung Galaxy S6 (123 x 79 x 11 mm) you hardly realize you’re carrying a complete computer with you. Use Sirius B wherever and whenever you want. Connect an HDMI screen, the power adapter and optionally a mouse and/or keyboard and you are ready to go!

Light as a Baseball

We know that no one likes to carry around heavy stuff. That’s why Ockel Sirius B weighs only 120 grams (or 4.23 ounces). This is less than the latest iPhone and the same as a couple of slices of bread. Or a baseball.

Connectivity Matters

The Ockel Sirius B is equipped with two USB 2.0 ports, an HDMI port, a Micro SD-card slot and a 3.5mm audio and microphone jack. That means you can connect a keyboard, mouse, speaker or any other external device within the blink of an eye. Would you prefer to enjoy the Sirius B without any cables? No problem, the Wi-Fi and Bluetooth chips will do the job.

Unbeatable Features

Whether it’s Netflix, Twitter, Flipboard or Dropbox: Ockel Sirus B handles it all. The millions of applications at the Windows 10 Appstore will give you the control (or fun) you are looking for.

  • Windows 10
  • 100% Silent
  • Plug & Play

The Ockel Sirius B runs on Windows 10. We offer you a perk with the popular Microsoft operating system pre-installed. Would you prefer to do the job yourself? Get one of our 'NO OS’ (No operating system) perks for a lower price, so you can install the operating system of your preference.

We designed the Ockel Sirius B in such a way that ventilation fans are no longer required. This unique feature combined with flash storage means you can enjoy working in silence and benefit from ultra-low power consumption. You might say that this white pocket PC is pretty green.

Step 1: Connect the Ockel Sirius B to an HDMI cable and connect the cable to a screen.

Step 2: Connect the power cable to the Sirius B and a power outlet.

Step 3: Start working or playing.
